### Summary

This research overview details a collaborative effort at the Stanford AIMI Center to utilize a massive new dataset of **COVID-19 chest x-rays and electronic health records** for predictive medical modeling. By integrating high-resolution medical imaging with structured lab values and metadata, the project establishes a rigorous framework for studying **clinical outcomes and diagnostic accuracy** during the pandemic.
